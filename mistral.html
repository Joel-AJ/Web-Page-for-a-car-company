About Mistral

The Bugatti W16 Mistral is a breathtaking roadster that represents the pinnacle of Bugatti’s legendary W16 engine series, combining open-top exhilaration with unmatched luxury and performance. Named after the powerful winds of southern France, the Mistral is designed to deliver an exhilarating driving experience with the wind in your hair, all while showcasing the unrivaled craftsmanship Bugatti is known for. This masterpiece is equipped with Bugatti’s iconic 8.0-liter W16 quad-turbocharged engine, capable of producing an astonishing 1,578 horsepower, making it the most powerful roadster Bugatti has ever built. The Mistral can reach a top speed of over 260 mph, offering a thrilling combination of speed and open-air freedom. The exterior design of the W16 Mistral is both bold and elegant, with aerodynamic elements optimized for high-speed stability. The front end features a more aggressive interpretation of Bugatti’s signature horseshoe grille, flanked by sharp, angular LED headlights, while the sleek, flowing lines of the body emphasize the car's elegance and purpose. The rear of the Mistral is dominated by an X-shaped light signature and a dynamic rear wing that enhances downforce when needed, providing both visual drama and functional performance. Inside, the Mistral exudes opulence, with an interior crafted from the finest materials, including soft leather, carbon fiber, and polished aluminum accents. The cockpit is designed to provide a driver-focused experience, with cutting-edge technology and intuitive controls within easy reach. Despite its race-inspired performance, the Mistral offers the utmost in comfort and refinement, making it a perfect grand tourer for long, scenic drives. Limited to only 99 units, the Bugatti W16 Mistral is a celebration of the brand’s engineering excellence and a tribute to the legendary W16 engine. It is not just a hypercar, but a symbol of exclusivity and a testament to Bugatti’s commitment to pushing the boundaries of automotive innovation.
